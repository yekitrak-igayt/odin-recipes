Curry

Description

Kadhi is prepared in various ways across North and Western Indian cuisines. While the choice of spices and herbs may vary by region, the base remains consistentâ€”a yogurt sauce thickened with gram flour, known as besan in Hindi. Besan, made from skinned black chickpeas, adds a distinctive nutty flavor. Traditionally, sour curd is used in kadhi, giving the dish its signature tangy, spiced, and creamy taste.

Ingredients

  • Curd
  • Besan
  • Garam Masala Powder
  • Salt
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Onion
  • Mustard Oil
  • Ginger
  • Green Chillies
  • Coriander

Steps

For making Curd Slurry

  1. In a bowl add sour curd and stir it well till it gets a smooth texture.
  2. Add Besan (majorly) with garam masala powder and salt to the curd.
  3. Stir and mix everything again.
  4. Add water in the mixture and stir it again till no lumps are left.

For making Pakora

  1. Take Besan in a bowl and add red chilli powder,garam masala and salt as required in it.
  2. Now add chopped onion in it and mix the contents well in the bowl. Wait for 30 minutes.
  3. Now add water in the bowl to get a thick batter.(can be skipped if the mixture already has too much moisture)
  4. Heat the oil in a pan and drop the batter in the oil.
  5. Fry the Pakora's till they are crisp and golden.
  6. Put the Pakora away now in a plate.

For making Curry

  1. In a pan heat a bit of mustard oil and add cumin seed in it.
  2. When the seeds starts to crackle add chopped onions. Stir and saute it.
  3. After some time add ginger,and green chillies.
  4. After a min or two add curry leaves and Dry red chillies.Stir and saute it.
  5. Then add the previously prepared Curd Slurry into the mixture.Stir it.
  6. Keep boiling the Curry and keep stiring it at medium flames for 15-30mins.More the time more the taste.
  7. After it starts boiling lower the heat and simmer it. The curry will thicken as it gets cooked and simmered and hot water if it becomes too thick
  8. Now add onion and pakora to the curry. while string gently.
  9. Let the pakora be soaked for 5-6 minutes.
  10. Lastly add the garam masala powder in the curry

Enjoy your Meal!
