Allo Pyaz ka Parantha

Description

Aloo Pyaaz Paratha is a popular North Indian comfort food that combines the warmth of mashed potatoes with the crunch of finely chopped onions, all seasoned with spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. This tasty filling is wrapped in whole wheat dough, rolled into thin discs, and cooked until golden and crispy on a griddle or tawa. Served hot, these parathas are often paired with tangy pickle, creamy yogurt, or a dollop of fresh butter, making it a satisfying dish for any time of the day.

Ingredients

For Dough

  • Wheat flour
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Ghee

For Stuffying

  • Potatoe
  • Onion
  • Green Chilles
  • Garam Masala Powder
  • Coriander Leaves
  • Salt

Steps

For Dough Making

  1. Take a bowl and mix wheat floour salt and ghee in it.
  2. Gradually add water and knead the dough until it softens.
  3. Brush the ghee on the dough

For Stuffing Making

  1. Combine ingridients in another bowl with mashed potatoes, chopped onions, green chillies, garam masala powder, chopped coriander leavesand salt.

For Allo-Pyaz Parantha Making

  1. Make 2 small dough ball and flatten them to a circular shape.
  2. Add the stuffing in b/w the circular shape and seal the stuffing with the other dough.
  3. Flatten the stuffed dough gently and sprinkle a bit of flour on top of it.
  4. Heat a tawa(pan) in medium heat and place the rolled out paratha on it.
  5. Cook the parantha until golden brown spot apear on it on one side. Do the same with other side.
  6. Add some ghee on the cooked side and cook it until it gets crispy.

Enjoy your Meal!
