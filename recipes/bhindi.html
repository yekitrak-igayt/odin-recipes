Bhindi

Description

Bhindi is one of those rare green vegetables loved by almost everyone. Prepared with a mild, spiced tempering, it's one of the quickest sabzis to whip up and a staple in many Indian kitchens. You can choose to make it dry or with gravy, depending on whether you're serving it with rice or rotiâ€”it's totally up to you!

Ingredients

  • Ladyfinger
  • Onion
  • Ghee
  • Haldi(tumeric powder)
  • Coriander
  • Jeera
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Grind coriander and jeera in a spice grinder.Set it aside
  2. Heat the ghee in a non sticky pan on medium flame. Add onions.Cook it until soft.
  3. Now add jeera. When jeera crackles add haldi, coriander. Toast it until fragrant.
  4. Add in chopped ladyfinger and cook for 5-6 minutes. When cooked to a crispy texture turn of the flame. Add a bit of salt for taste.
  5. Garnish the food with yogort and plain roti.

Enjoy your Meal!
